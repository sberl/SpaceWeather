GOES X-ray Flux

The red line on the graph indicates the level of solar activity that will affect Earth. Using the solar x-ray activity scale (A,B,C,M, and X), determine the power of the solar storm.

Scientists have created five levels; A, B, C, M, and X to help determine if a CME has occurred.

  • A is the lowest level
  • B is 10 times more powerful than A
  • C is 10 times more powerful than B
  • M is 10 times more powerful than C
  • X is 10 times stronger than M

X-type flares are often CMES.

ACE Solar Wind

The graph shows the solar wind's effects on Earth's magnetosphere.

Each time the yellow line in the graph drops below the dashed white line labeled '0', we know that our magnetosphere has been affected by the solar wind.

Look at the most recent 6 hours of data and locate the lowest points on the graph.

Use the following levels to determine the intensity of the storm:

  • Low (0 to -2)
  • Medium (-2 to -4)
  • High (-4 to -6)

More Information:

This is a map of the magnetic field of Mars observed by the Mars Global Surveyor satellite.
Which areas have the most intense magnetic fields?

Red and blue stripes represent magnetic fields with opposite directions. Darker hues represent more intense magnetic fields.

If the area is located below the equator (zero degrees latitude) it is located in the southern hemisphere, if it is above the equator it is in the northern hemisphere.

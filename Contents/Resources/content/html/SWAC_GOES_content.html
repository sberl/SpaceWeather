GOES X-Ray Flux

The red line on the graph indicates the level of solar activity. Scientists have created a scale to help determine if a CME has occurred.

Use the scale to determine the power of the solar storm.

  • A is the lowest level
  • B is 10 times more powerful than A
  • C is 10 times more powerful than B
  • M is 10 times more powerful than C
  • X is 10 times stronger than M

X-type flares are often CMES.

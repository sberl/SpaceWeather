Mars Surface and CMEs

More Information:

Even with heavy shielding the Mars Control station can be affected by the plasma cloud of electrically charged particles from a coronal mass ejection (CME) since there is no protection from a strong magnetosphere on Mars.

Robotic spacecraft on the surface could be affected. Cameras could malfunction, communications equipment might be damaged and computer data may be corrupted. Crews on spacewalks would return to the spacecraft.

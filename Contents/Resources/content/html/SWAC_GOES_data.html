GOES X-Ray Flux

The solar x--ray activity scale determines the intensity of the solar storm. A is the lowest level, B is 10 times more powerful than A, C is 10 times more powerful than B, M is 10 times more powerful than C, and X is 10 times stronger than M. So this makes an X event 10,000 times stronger than A. An X-class event is often a CME.

About the ACE Mission

The Advanced Composition Explorer (ACE) satellite monitors the speed, composition and magnetic field of the solar wind. The ACE satellite is always directly between Earth and the Sun. ACE is located at the LaGrange 1 point, a point of gravitational balance located approximately 1 million miles from the Earth. The ACE data includes the time the solar wind arrives at ACE. It can provide advance warning of the arrival of a CME.

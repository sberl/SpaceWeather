Kp Index

The larger the Kp index the more active the Earth's magnetic field becomes due to a solar flare or CME. This chart shows the Kp index from 0 to 9 on the vertical axis. The horizontal axis shows 3 hour estimated data.

GREEN BARS show a Kp index less than 4 - Little magnetic change.

YELLOW BARS show a Kp index equal to 4 - Moderate magnetic change.

RED BARS show when the Kp is greater than 4 - A storm warning.

Kp values greater than 7 indicate a large disturbance.

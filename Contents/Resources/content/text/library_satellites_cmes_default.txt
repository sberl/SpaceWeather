		<h2>Satellites and CMEs</h2>
	</div>
	
	<div id="term-content">
		
		<div id="full-pane">
		
			<img class="right" src="../images/library_satellites_cmes.jpg" alt="Satellites and CMEs" />
		   
			<span id="more-information">
				<p>
				Satellites are vulnerable to energetic solar particles such as those from <strong><a href="event:loadToolCItem('Research','ResearchGeomagneticStormhtm')">geomagnetic storm</a></strong> caused by a <strong><a href="event:loadToolCItem('Research','ResearchCoronalMassEjectionhtm')">coronal mass ejection (CME)</a></strong>. 
				</p>
				<p>
				Geomagnetic storms create conditions that lead to enhanced charging of satellite surfaces.  
				</p>
				<p>
This can lead to degradation of solar panels and discharges that lead to false-commands and data corruption in computers, and electronic problems with communications components.
				</p>
			</span>

<p>The plasma cloud from a CME can damage electronic systems on the Spacecraft. As a precautionary measure you will need to retract the antenna.</p>
<ol>
	<li>Click on the <strong>Antenna</strong> button on the right side of the screen.</li>
	<li>Follow the instructions on the screen. The antenna retract code is <strong>786</strong>.</li>
	<li>Close the Antenna control panel by clicking on the X in the upper right corner of the window.</li>
	<li>Choose the next task when you have completed all of the steps in this task.</li>
</ol>

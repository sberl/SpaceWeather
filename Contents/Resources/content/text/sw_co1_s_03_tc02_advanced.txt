<p>You will need to calculate the speed of the CME.</p>
<ol>
	<li>Click on the <strong>CME</strong> button on the right side of the screen.</li>
	<li>Follow the instructions on the screen and use the calculator (<strong>CALC</strong> button at the top of the screen). If you need help, ask the <strong>Mission Commander</strong>.</li>
	<li>Answer <strong>QUESTION 2</strong> on the <strong>CME</strong> section of your <strong>DATA LOG</strong>.</li> 
	<li>Send this message to Mission Control: "<strong>The CME is travelling at _________ kilometers per hour</strong>."</li>
	<li>Close the CME window by clicking on the X in the upper right corner of the window.</li>
	<li>Go to <strong>Step 3</strong>.</li>
</ol>

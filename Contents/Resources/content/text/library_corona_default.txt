	<div id="term">
		<h2>Corona</h2>
	</div>
	
	<div id="term-content">
		
		<div id="full-pane">
		
			<span id="definition">
				<p>
					<strong>Definition:</strong><br />
					The outermost layer of the Sun's atmosphere, visible to the eye during a total solar eclipse; it can also be observed through special filters 
					and by X-ray cameras aboard satellites. The corona is very hot, up to 1-1.5 million degrees centigrade, and is the source of the solar wind. 
				</p>
			</span>

	<h1>Kp Index</h1>
	<p>
		The larger the Kp index the more active Earth's magnetic field becomes due to a solar flare or CME.  This chart shows the Kp index from 0 to 9 on the vertical axis. The horizontal axis shows 3 hour estimated data.
	</p>
	<p>
		GREEN BARS show a Kp index less than 4 - <strong>Little magnetic change</strong>.
	</p>
	<p>
		YELLOW BARS show a Kp index equal to 4 - <strong>Moderate magnetic change</strong>.
	</p>
	<p>
		RED BARS show when the Kp is greater than 4  - <strong>A storm warning</strong>.
	</p>
	<p>
		<strong>Kp values greater than 7 indicate a large disturbance.</strong>
	</p>

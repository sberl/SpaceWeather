	<p>
		<strong>How fast is the CME traveling?</strong> 
	</p>
	<ol>
		<li>Divide the distance the flare traveled in kilometers by the time it took in minutes.</li>
		<li>Multiply your answer by 60 to get kilometers per hour.</li>
	</ol>
	<p>
		This is the speed of the coronal mass ejection.
	</p>

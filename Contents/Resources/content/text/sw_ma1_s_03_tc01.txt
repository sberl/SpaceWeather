<p>You need to determine if the CME is heading towards Earth and the Mars Lander.</p>
<ol>
	<li>Click on the Space Weather Action Center (<strong>SWAC</strong>) on the right side of the screen.</li>
	<li>Under <strong>SUNSPOT REGIONS</strong> select <strong>Large Angle and Spectrometric Coronagraph (LASCO)</strong>.</li>
	<li>Play the movie.</li> 
	<li>Answer <strong>QUESTION 1</strong> on the <strong>CME</strong> section of your <strong>DATA LOG</strong>.</li>
	<li>If a storm is heading towards Earth and our spacecraft, send this message to Mars Control: "<strong>Satellite data indicates a solar flare is heading directly towards the Spacecraft</strong>."</li>
	<li>Close SWAC by clicking on the X in the upper right corner of the window.</li>
	<li>Go to <strong>Step 2</strong>.</li>
</ol>

<p>You need to determine when the CME will arrive in order to complete a Space Weather Report.</p>
<ol>
	<li>Click on the <strong>CME</strong> button on the right side of the screen.</li>
	<li>Follow the instructions on the screen and use the calculator (<strong>CALC</strong> button at the top of the screen). Answer <strong>QUESTION 1</strong> on the <strong>CME</strong> section of your <strong>DATA LOG</strong> as you go. If you need help ask the <strong>Flight Director</strong>.</li>
	<li>Inform the <strong>Flight Director</strong> when the CME will arrive and recommend an announcement.</li>
	<li>Close the CME window by clicking on the X in the upper right corner of the window.</li>
	<li>Go to <strong>Step 2</strong>.</li>	
</ol>

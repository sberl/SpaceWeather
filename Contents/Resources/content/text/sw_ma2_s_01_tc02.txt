<p>To download satellite data, you must first deploy the antenna on the outside of the Spacecraft.</p>
<ol>
	<li>Click on the <strong>Antenna</strong> button on the right side of the screen.</li> 
	<li>Follow the instructions on the screen. The antenna deploy code is <strong>324</strong>.</li> 
	<li>Close the Antenna control panel by clicking on the X in the upper right corner of the window.</li>
	<li>Go to <strong>Step 3</strong>.</li>
</ol>

<p>Aurora are visible signs that a solar storm has occurred.</p>
<ol>
	<li>Click on the Space Weather Action Center (<strong>SWAC</strong>) on the right side of the screen.</li>
	<li>Under <strong>AURORA</strong> select <strong>Kiruna All-Sky Camera</strong>.</li>
	<li>Observe the image for any signs of auroral activity.</li> 
	<li>Answer <strong>QUESTION 1</strong> on the <strong>AURORA</strong> section of your <strong>DATA LOG</strong>.</li>
	<li>Close SWAC by clicking on the X in the upper right corner of the window.</li>
	<li>Go to <strong>Step 2</strong>.</li>
</ol>

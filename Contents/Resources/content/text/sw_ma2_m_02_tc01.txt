<p>You will need to check the system status of the Mars Lander, Mars orbital satellites and Mars control to see if any systems were damaged by the solar storm.</p>
<ol>
	<li>Click on the <strong>Systems Status</strong> button on the right side of the screen and select <strong>MARS LANDER</strong>.</li>
	<li>Look at the light (green or red) for each system and note any warning messages.</li>
	<li>Answer <strong>QUESTION 1</strong> on the <strong>CME EFFECTS</strong> section of your <strong>DATA LOG</strong>.</li>
	<li>Inform the <strong>Flight Director</strong> if any action is required.</li>
	<li>Go to <strong>Step 2</strong>.</li>
</ol>

<p>The Kp Index Map shows the connection between the Kp index (a measure of Earth's magnetic field) and the predicted edges of the aurora.</p>
<ol>
	<li>Under <strong>AURORA</strong> select <strong>Kp Index Map</strong>. </li>
	<li>The Kp Index for the last eight hours is <strong>eight</strong>.  Based on this level, answer <strong>QUESTION 3</strong> on the <strong>AURORA</strong> section of your <strong>DATA LOG</strong>.</li> 
	<li>Send this message to Mission Control: "<strong>Aurora are predicted in the night sky on the following continents: _______________. Check the ground-based cameras</strong>."</li>
	<li>Close SWAC by clicking on the X in the upper right corner of the window.
	<li>Choose the next task when you have completed all of the steps in this task.</li>

<p>The Space Weather Report is an update for the crew in the Spacecraft and the team in Mission Control.</p>
<ol>
	<li>Locate the <strong>SPACE WEATHER REPORT</strong> in your <strong>DATA LOG</strong>.</li>
	<li>Fill in the locations of aurora sightings you received from the Spacecraft and your System Status information. If you have not received the information, send this to the Spacecraft: "<strong>Please send  the continents on which aurora will be seen.</strong>"</li>
	<li>Go to the <strong>COM TEAM</strong> and give them the report to read.</li>
	<li>Choose the next task when you have completed all of the steps in this task.</li>
</ol>

<p>We can overlay an ultraviolet image on top of a magnetogram. This is called a composite, which can illustrate areas of activity on the Sun.</p>
<ol>
	<li>Under <strong>SUNSPOT REGIONS</strong> select <strong>HMI/AIA Composite Image</strong>.</li>
	<li>Answer <strong>QUESTION 1</strong> on the <strong>SOLAR OBSERVATIONS</strong> section of your <strong>DATA LOG</strong>.</li>
	<li>Click on the <strong>MOVIE</strong> button to watch the solar flare movie.</li>
	<li>Send this message to Mission Control: <strong>"We have observed _____ active sunspot(s)."</strong></li>
	<li>Close SWAC by clicking on the X in the upper right corner of the window.</li>
	<li>Choose the next task when you have completed all of the steps in this task.</li>
</ol>

<p>Your team needs to determine which types of data help scientists to predict a space weather event by completing a Final Analysis.</p>
<ol>
	<li>Click on the <strong>Final Analysis</strong> button on the right side of the screen.</li>
	<li>Follow the instructions on the screen. Make sure you check your analysis before you send it.</li>
	<li>Click on the <strong>SEND REPORT TO MISSION COMMANDER</strong> button when you have finished the first part of your analysis.</li>
	<li>Click on <strong>GO TO PART 2</strong>. Follow the instructions on screen. Make sure you check your analysis before you send it.</li>
	<li>Click on the <strong>SEND REPORT TO MISSION COMMANDER</strong> button when you have finished.</li>
	<li>Close the Final Analysis window by clicking on the X in the upper right corner of the window.</li>
	<li>Inform the <strong>Mission Commander</strong> that you have completed all of your tasks. You are now free to explore the LIBRARY to learn more about Space Weather.</li>
</ol>

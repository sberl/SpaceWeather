<p>To determine where aurora on Mars may be observed you need to access a map of Mars' magnetic fields.</p>
<ol>
	<li>Click on <strong>LIBRARY</strong> button at the top of the screen.</li>
	<li>Look up <strong>Mars' Magnetic Fields</strong>. Read the information.</li>
	<li>Answer <strong>QUESTION 3</strong> on the <strong>AURORA</strong> section of your <strong>DATA LOG</strong>.</li>
	<li>Send this message to Mars Control: "<strong>Mars auroras are predicted in the night sky in the ____________ hemisphere of Mars. Check satellite data</strong>."</li>
	<li>Close LIBRARY by clicking on the X in the upper right corner of the window.</li>
	<li>Choose the next task when you have completed all of the steps in this task.</li>
</ol>

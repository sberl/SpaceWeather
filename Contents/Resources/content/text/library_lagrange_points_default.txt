		<h2>Lagrange Points, L-Points or liberation points</h2>

			<span id="definition">
				<p>
					<strong>Definition:</strong><br />
					Lagrange points, L-points, or libration points are the five positions in an orbital configuration where a small object affected only by gravity is stationary relative to two larger objects (such as <strong><em>satellite</em></strong> with respect to the Earth and Sun.)

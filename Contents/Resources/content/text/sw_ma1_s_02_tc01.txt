<p>To make observations of the Sun you need to access satellite data. To access this data, you must deploy the antenna on the outside of the spacecraft.</p>
<ol>
	<li>Click the <strong>Antenna</strong> button on the right side of the screen.</li>  
	<li>Follow the instructions on the screen.  The antenna deploy code is <strong>324</strong>.</li>
	<li>Close the Antenna control panel by clicking the X in the upper right corner of the window.</li>
	<li>Go to <strong>Step 2</strong>.</li>
</ol>

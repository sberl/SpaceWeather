		<h2>Magnetosphere</h2>
	</div>
	
	<div id="term-content">
	
		<div id="full-pane">
		
			<span id="definition">
				<p>
					<strong>Definition:</strong><br />
					The region of space in which the <strong><em>magnetic field</em></strong> of an object (e.g., a star or planet) counters the pressure of the stellar wind.  Earth's magnetosphere: A region of space surrounding Earth that shields the Earth from the solar wind.
				</p>

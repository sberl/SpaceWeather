	<div id="term">
		<h2>Satellites and CMEs</h2>
	</div>
	<div id="term-content">

		<img class="right" src="../images/library_satellites_cmes_mars.jpg" alt="Satellites and CMEs" />
		<p>
			The energetic solar particles in the plasma cloud of a coronal mass ejection (CME) can create conditions that lead to electrical charging of satellite surfaces.
		</p>
		<p>
			This can cause damage to solar panels, data corruption in computers, and electronic problems with communications.
		</p>

	<h1>SPEED CALCULATION</h1>
	
	<p><strong>How fast is the CME travelling?</strong></p>
	<ol>
		<li>Divide the distance the flare traveled (your Distance Measurement) by the time it took (your Time Measurement).</li><br />
		<li>Multiply your answer by 60 to get kilometers per hour.</li>
	</ol>

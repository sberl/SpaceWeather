<p>Your team needs to review the procedures for a space debris or meteor shower emergency and inform the Spacecraft.</p>
<ol>
	<li>Click on the <strong>Debris Procedures</strong> button on the right side of the screen.</li>
	<li>Read the information.</li>
	<li>Send this message to the Spacecraft: "<strong>Warning: The spacecraft is encountering debris. Follow the procedures in your DEBRIS task cards</strong>."</li>
	<li>Close the Debris Procedures Window by clicking on the X in the upper right corner of the window.</li>
	<li>Inform the <strong>Flight Director</strong> that you have sent the procedures to the Spacecraft.</li>
	<li>Go to <strong>Step 2</strong>.</li>
</ol>
